Frequently Asked Questions

Most solar panels come with a 25-30 year warranty and can continue producing electricity beyond that timeframe, though at slightly reduced efficiency. With proper maintenance, your solar system can last 35+ years.

Standard grid-tied systems shut down during outages for safety. However, with a battery backup system, you can continue using solar power during outages. We can design a system with battery storage if this is important to you.

Solar panels require minimal maintenance. We recommend occasional cleaning if you live in a dusty area and annual inspections to ensure optimal performance. Most systems can be monitored remotely to detect any issues.

Yes, solar panels still generate electricity on cloudy days, though at reduced output (typically 10-25% of full capacity). Modern panels are quite efficient at capturing diffuse sunlight. Your system is designed to account for your local weather patterns.
